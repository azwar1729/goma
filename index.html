Biography

I am a first year Master's student in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University, West Lafayette. I'm presently working on Neural Speech Decoding, as a Research Assistant with Prof. Joseph Makin. In Spring 2021, I was a Teaching Assistant for CNIT 15501, an undergraduate course on Python Programming, with Prof. Tawfiq Salem.

I completed my undergradute degree from IIT Madras in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Data Science. I later worked as a Data and Applied Scientist at AI and Research, Microsoft, Bengaluru office for a year and half. At Microsoft, I build frameworks for detection, rejection and removal of adversarial attacks on multi-media advertising such as Product and Text Ads displayed anywhere by Microsoft that violates Editorial Policies or poses risks from Fraud, Account Compromise and Spoofing.
